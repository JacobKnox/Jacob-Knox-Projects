Things to Do in Lakeland

Lake Mirror Complex

The Lake Mirror Complex offers a variety of park spaces and classes/activities. A few things you can do here are play bridge, ballroom and line dancing for seniors, get involved in the Stamp Club, and play in a concert band.

Sun n' Fun

"The SUN ‘n FUN Aerospace Expo is Florida’s largest annual convention of any kind, and the 2nd largest air show in the world. Join over 225,000 aviation enthusiast for 6 exciting days of cutting edge technology exhibits, forums, workshops, career fairs, and of course our world-famous breathtaking aerial displays by some of the best airshow performers in the business!" - Sun n' Fun Home Page

Polk Museum of Art

For those who enjoy art and learning, this is the perfect location! The whole mission of this museum is to educate people. With more than 2,800 pieces in their collection, you can expect to see pieces from Rembrandt van Rijn, Andy Warhol, Faith Ringgold, Pablo Picasso, Miriam Shapiro, James Rosenquist, Barbara Kruger, Marc Chagall, Damien Hirst, Hung Liu and others.
